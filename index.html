The Hype Guard Framework

Six point criteria to determine the value of your technical investments.

  • Alignment

    Does this technology solve a verified operational bottleneck, or is it a solution looking for a problem? I map tech capabilities directly to your core business objectives.

  • Value

    Quantifying the "Time-to-Competence." I evaluate the tangible efficiency gains, safety improvements, and data accuracy benefits against the cost of implementation.

  • Friction

    Identifying the "Human Hurdle." I assess workforce digital literacy, user interface barriers, and the likelihood of floor-level rejection before you deploy.

  • Governance

    Navigating the "German Works Council" (Betriebsrat), global HR policies, and data privacy laws. I ensure your tech rollout isn't halted by legal or regulatory friction.

  • Infrastructure

    Deep-dive analysis of your 3D data pipelines, Edge Computing, and network latency. If your pipes can't handle the data, the technology will fail.

  • Economics

    The brutal reality of TCO (Total Cost of Ownership). From hardware lifecycles to subscription bloat, I tell you if the investment is a financial win or a sinkhole.
